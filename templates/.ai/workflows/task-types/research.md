# Research Task Template

## Required Fields

- Question to answer.
- Decision the research should enable.
- Sources or repository areas to inspect.
- Timebox or stopping condition.

## Lifecycle Emphasis

- In `triage`, confirm that no code change is expected unless separately
  approved.
- In `planning`, list evidence needed and unknowns.
- In `delivery`, provide findings, options, tradeoffs, and recommendation.
- In `memory-update`, record only decisions or stable facts, not raw notes.
