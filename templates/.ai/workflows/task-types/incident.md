# Incident Task Template

## Required Fields

- Incident summary.
- Start time and detection source.
- User/customer impact.
- Current mitigation status.
- Systems involved.

## Lifecycle Emphasis

- In `triage`, prioritize mitigation and data safety over cleanup.
- In `execution`, preserve a timeline of hypotheses, actions, and evidence.
- In `validation`, prove mitigation worked and note remaining risk.
- In `memory-update`, record durable prevention lessons and follow-up actions.
